## OWASP AI Resources

| **Resource** | **Description** |
|---|---|
| [OWASP AI Exchange](https://owaspai.org/) | Comprehensive framework of AI threats, controls, and best practices. 300+ pages of practical guidance. |
| [OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-10-for-large-language-model-applications/) | The most widely adopted risk framework for LLM-powered applications (2025 edition). |
| [OWASP Top 10 for Agentic Applications](https://genai.owasp.org/resource/owasp-top-10-for-agentic-applications-for-2026/) | Emerging risks specific to autonomous AI agents (2026, peer-reviewed). |
| [OWASP MCP Top 10](https://owasp.org/www-project-mcp-top-10/) | Security risks in Model Context Protocol deployments (beta). |
| [OWASP AI Security & Privacy Guide](https://github.com/OWASP/www-project-ai-security-and-privacy-guide) | Practical guidance on securing AI systems and protecting data privacy. |
